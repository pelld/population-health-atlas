Notes and definitions,
1,"In 2023-24, the depression indicator group comprised of a single indicator, which was based on patients who have been newly diagnosed with depression in the preceding 1 April – 31 March or who had a new diagnosis of depression in the last 3 months of the previous QOF year and did not achieve in that QOF year. 

Consequently, the previous depression register (patients aged at least 18 years old whose latest unresolved episode of depression is since 1st April 2006) was removed from QOF as it was not required for the calculation of the Adjusted Practice Disease Factor (APDF) to support QOF payments in the 2023-24 reporting year.

As a result, QOF data 2023-24 could not be used to calculate the prevalence of depression. However, for 2024-25 the depression register has been reintroduced so prevalence for this year can therefore be calculated."
,
2,"Due to the change for 2023-24, noted in the above, incidence was published as it was not possible to calculate the prevalence. The incidence continues to be published for 2024-25, along with the prevalence for this year, now that the register has been reintroduced."
